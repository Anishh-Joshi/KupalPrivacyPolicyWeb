Kupal - Privacy Policy

Kuple is a Dating app for the students of Kathmandu University. When you use Kupal, you share some information with us. So we need to be direct with regards to the data we gather, how we use it, whom we share it with.

Information We Collect

There are two basic categories of information we collect:

  • Information that you provide.
  • Information we get when you use our Kupal.
Here's a little more detail on each of these categories.

Information You Provide

When you first install Kupal and create an account, you will be asked to fill out a form including things like your Email, Username, Name, University Faculty, University Year. If you choose to sign in by Google Account we additionally collect your email address, displayname, google profile photo.
We may also ask you to provide us with some additional information like enabling/disabling dating features and your account profile type. It will remain public and other users can see if your account is set to public or if you have enabled/disabled dating features.

Information We Get When You Use Our Services

When you use Kupal , we collect certain information. We collect these Informations from you -

  • General Information. We collect information about your activity through the firebase dashboard.
  • Device Information. We collect information from and about the devices you use. For example, we collect:
  • information about your hardware and software, such as the hardware model and operating system.
  • Camera and Photos. If you choose to upload a photo on Kupal you may have to give Camera Permission.
  • Location Information. If you choose to upload a photo on Kupal and add a location you may have to give Location Permission.
  • Notification Permission. If you choose to receive notifications like for messages and activity you may have to give notification permission.

How We Use Information

We use the information provided to suggest new users or view users' profiles.

How We Share Information

We do not share any kind of information with anybody.
